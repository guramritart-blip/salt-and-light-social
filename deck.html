Who We Are

Hi, we’re Sarah & G.

  • We care deeply about faith, people, and real community
  • We started this because genuine connection has become hard to find
  • We wanted to create something simple, human, and full of heart

Why Salt & Light Exists

Why this community
needed to exist.

  • People still want faith in their lives
  • People still want community in their lives
  • But those two things don’t always feel easy to find together
  • We believe they should intersect — with love at the center
♡  Love at the center

What Salt & Light Is — and Isn’t

This is.

  • A faith-rooted social community
  • A place to connect, belong, and build real friendships

This is not.

  • A church
  • A sermon series
  • A ministry program
  • Something people have to perform for

What Matters Most

What’s at the center
of everything we do.

  • ♡  Love first
  • Kindness over judgment
  • Real community over surface-level connection
  • A safe and welcoming environment
  • Space for people from different backgrounds to feel at home

Hobby Groups

Hobby groups are built
by the community.

  • Hobby groups are a big part of how this community grows
  • If there is something you already love doing, let us know
  • We can help start that conversation in GroupMe
  • If you want to lead a hobby group, we can help point people toward you
  • This is meant to be shaped by the people in it, not just attended
Initiative matters ✦

Shared Ownership

Everyone has
a part to play.

  • Big gatherings will be planned and led by Sarah & G
  • But community does not only happen at big gatherings
  • Members can take initiative in their own way
  • That can look like leading a hobby group
  • Or helping create connection in their local city
  • Or simply being someone who makes others feel welcome
This community is yours to shape.

City Chats

Local connection
matters.

  • The purpose of the city chats is to help you connect with people in your area

  • Some people want a big gathering
  • Some people want a smaller local circle
  • We want to make room for both
