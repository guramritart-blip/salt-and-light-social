WHO WE ARE

Faith-rooted. Human-centered. Fun. ✨

Salt & Light Social is a community for good people who want to get back to real life with real people who really get it. We're faith-driven with humanitarian-focused values — and we believe community starts with fun.

We're here for the humanists, the marginalized, the POCs, the immigrants — and everyone who has a good heart and has been looking for their people again.
