👋 Meet the Founders

We can't wait to meet you!

Co-Founder

Sarah Mehta

Sarah is an Orange County native who grew up rooted in faith, service, and community. For over a decade, she served in ministry and built her life within the church she called home for more than 20 years. When that community shifted in ways that no longer felt like home, Sarah made the difficult decision to step away, losing not just a church, but a deeply woven circle of friendships and belonging.

Rather than letting that loss define the story, she felt called to rebuild. And she wasn't alone in that longing. Her boyfriend G was searching for the same thing: a new couple navigating faith together, looking for others who still held genuine humanist values in a season of growing division. Over a few late nights of Reddit threads and website building, Salt & Light came to life, not as a polished project but as an honest answer to something they both felt was missing.

Outside of building community, Sarah's background spans strategy and consulting with PwC, NASA, and the Department of the Interior. In her free time you'll find her singing, skateboarding, cooking, or at a cozy coffee shop with a matcha or very strong latte in hand. She's a proud cat mom to Claire and has a soft spot for springtime blooms and the coziest Christmas season. 🎄

If you ask Sarah what her favorite thing is, it's caring for others alongside G, teaming up to pour encouragement into the people around them who could use a little more love and light in a divisive world.

Co-Founder

Guramrit Malhotra

G came to America from India in 2017 with not much more than a camera, a college acceptance letter, and an appetite for something he couldn't yet name. He studied arts and film at UC Irvine, where his eye for people, light, and story took real shape. Today he works as a photographer, filmmaker, and director, spending his days on shoots, in the edit bay, and in creative meetings.

About a year ago, something shifted. G describes a season where life felt detached, where worldly things had quietly taken too much of his attention. Through the people God placed in his path, a few key friendships and a love that changed everything, his heart began to open. Faith became real, not as a concept but as something he could feel. In the way his perspective softened. In the way his relationships with family started to heal. In the way the ordinary world around him started to look a little brighter.

He will tell you he is still figuring it out. But there is a warmth and a groundedness to him that people notice right away.

Outside of work, G is the person who will stay up talking through any idea you put in front of him. He loves a long drive, has a serious thing for colognes, and has recently been spending more time in the kitchen. And if you ask him what his favorite thing is, he'll tell you it's spending time with Sarah, especially reading together.

He co-founded Salt & Light with his partner Sarah because community matters, and because some things are better figured out together.
