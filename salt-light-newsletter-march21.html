Hello everyone!

It's Sunday and we're so excited to kick off the week preparing for our first event this Saturday!

Details are finalized and we can't wait to meet you all and officially begin building our first Salt & Light community together. Everyone has been incredible with their support, follow-ups, and encouragement as we've been working hard to get this group off the ground.

Since this is our first gathering, we'll be covering some important details for the future of the group that you won't want to miss, including: how we'll communicate moving forward, connecting attendees with others in their local cities, and forming our first hobby groups (outdoor sports, crafting, movies, book clubs, etc.) Most importantly, it will be a chance to get to know one another and start forming real community friendships.

We've been deeply moved by the outpouring of support from so many of you who have shared similar experiences, feeling displaced from churches, friendships, or communities that once felt like home. Salt & Light was created by two people who experienced that loss and thought, "There must be others out there going through the same thing, and we need to find them."

This group is more than just a social club. It's a place to seek understanding, refuge, and connection. A space where relationships can grow without fear of division or judgment. Our goal is simple: to remind people what it truly means to live out the values of faith. Loving, kind, supportive, protective, and committed to the dignity and value of every person.

You don't have to be Christian to be part of this. If you resonate with these human-centered values, you're already on the same team.

We're simply looking for people who want to be around others who support one another, people who want to get back to life, fun, and hope again.

If that sounds like you, we can't wait to meet you this Saturday. You're going to meet some really great people.

And we're incredibly excited about that.
