Meet G & Sarah

Guramrit (G)
Co-Founder

G is an entrepreneur, photographer, and community builder based in Southern California. After years of building things for others, he turned his focus to something more personal — a community where people could find real connection outside the noise of everyday life. Salt & Light is the group he always wished existed.

Sarah
Co-Founder

Sarah is a strategic operations leader who has worked across enterprise, government, and nonprofit sectors. She brings structure, warmth, and deep intentionality to everything she does. For Sarah, Salt & Light is about rebuilding the kind of community that reminds us all what it means to truly show up for one another.

"We built this because we experienced the loss of community firsthand — and knew others were going through the same thing."

Who We Are

What is
Salt & Light?

Salt & Light Social is a community group for people who believe in real connection — those who value kindness, faith-inspired living, and showing up for one another.

We Are

  • A space to seek understanding and connection
  • A place where relationships grow without judgment
  • Rooted in love, kindness, and human dignity
  • Open to all who share these values

We Are Not

  • A church or religious institution
  • A dating group
  • Exclusive to any denomination or background
  • A place for division or debate

"You don't have to be Christian to be here. If you resonate with loving, supportive values — you're already on the same team."

Structure

How We're
Organized

Salt & Light is built around three layers of community — so there's always a place that feels the right size for you.

🌐 Salt & Light Main Group
The full community. All members, all announcements, big events, and the heartbeat of the organization. This is home base — everyone belongs here.
🏙 City Groups
Smaller, local clusters based on where you live or hang out. More intimate, easier to coordinate local meetups and spontaneous plans with people nearby.
🎯 Hobby Groups
Groups built around shared interests — hiking, film, food, fitness, books, and more. Member-led and open to anyone in the community who wants to join.

All groups live within our GroupMe community. Join the main group first — city and hobby groups will be introduced as we grow.
