Good Afternoon & Welcome Friends!

After reaching out to the Reddit community with our post titled "Non-Maga Social Group?" and where we asked OC Reddit community members if they would have an interest in me starting a human-centric social group for Non-Maga Christians (and of course our humanist friends) — we received an outpouring of almost 400 upvotes on the post and 105 comments asking us to PLEASE get this group going!

With that response, we're getting the ball rolling.

WHO WE ARE

New, innovational, and intentional ✨

Our group is new, innovational, and intentional. We're starting this thing by being founded on true, accurate Christ-centered beliefs that all people are equal, everyone is valuable, and human rights are sacred. Where faith starts with fun and divisive politics are thrown out the window.

We're faith-driven people with humanitarian-focused values. We value safety, security, and the preservation of what is good, loving, and neighborly. And we're here to get back into our hobbies and have some fun together.

OUR VISION

A group for everyone with a heart of gold 🤝

We're a group for the humanists, the marginalized, the POCs, the immigrants, and everyone who has a heart of gold looking for the light again.

Our vision is to get back to community, connecting people with their unknown neighbors who also believe in getting back to a world where we build deep relationships, encourage one another, go outside, play tennis, knit a beanie, read books, see movies, talk about the deep things of life, hang out and just have fun together.

WHY THIS MATTERS

You're not alone in this 💜

We want to remove the fear of searching for new friendships by creating a space where you feel understood and known. In essence, we're all trauma-bond healing — and that's okay.

We've all struggled watching our churches, our friend groups, our families, and our communities we've built relationships in for years all seem to topple overnight. It's left many of us feeling lost wondering where we can go to get back to the core ideals of humanity again.

We're all searching for a community where we can find friends who we trust are on the same page, know basic right from wrong, believe in protecting humanity, and stand up against acts of hatred, violence, and lies that destroy our hope. We're here with a desire to create that safe space, and we want you to be a part of creating it too.

THE ANNOUNCEMENT

WE ARE ON!!! 🎉

Welcome to Salt & Light Social

Where real humans get back to real life, with real people, who really get it.

With such a positive response from the Reddit community, we are pleased to announce our grassroots community group is officially happening! Our vision is to be a community-first social group that creates sub-platforms for the fun activities YOU want to get back to with the cool people within your city.

WHAT'S NEXT

Our first meeting is coming! 👉

We're excited to announce we'll be hosting our first meeting within the next 2 weeks and we want YOU to come!

During this meeting we'll have an opportunity to socialize with one another, talk about our vision for the group, plan upcoming events, and get this thing off the ground!

We're currently tallying how many folks we have coming and locating a space in central Irvine to host our first group meeting. We received responses from people in cities as south as San Clemente and as north as Garden Grove/LA — so we figured to accommodate commute for everyone, Irvine is the most central location.

In the meantime…

We're also working hard to create our first Discord group and website where we'll keep everyone updated on what we're doing next and how to be involved. While these things are still in draft phase, we invite you to come visit them and learn more about this cool new community we're creating together.
